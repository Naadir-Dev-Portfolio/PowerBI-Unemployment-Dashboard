# PowerBI-Unemployment-Dashboard

> Power BI dashboard tracking UK unemployment trends, claimant counts, and labour market shifts across regions and demographic groups.

![PowerBI-Unemployment-Dashboard](repo-card.png)

Built by [Naadir](https://github.com/Naadir-Dev-Portfolio)

---

## Overview

Labour market analytics dashboard built on official ONS and DWP data. It tracks how unemployment and economic inactivity rates have moved over time, how the picture varies by region and age group, and what the current labour market signals suggest. Published as a live interactive report accessible directly via the link below.

---

## Features

- National unemployment and claimant count trend analysis over time
- Regional breakdown highlighting labour market variation across the UK
- Demographic split by age group and economic activity status
- DAX measures for unemployment rate, claimant rate, and period-over-period change
- Live interactive version published on Power BI service

---

## Tech Stack

`Power BI` · `DAX` · `Power Query` · `PBIP`
